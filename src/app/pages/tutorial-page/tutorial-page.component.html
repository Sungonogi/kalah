How to Play

Kalah is a two-player strategy game played on a board consisting of two rows of pits and two larger pits, one at each end, called stores. Each player controls one row of pits and the store on their right. The goal of the game is to collect more points in your store than your opponent.

Gameplay

Each pit begins with an equal number of stones, and the stores are empty. Players take turns picking all stones from one of their pits and distributing them counterclockwise into pits and stores, skipping the opponent's store.

Special Moves

  • Extra Move: If the last stone lands in the player's own store, they get an additional turn.
  • Capture: If the last stone lands in an empty pit on your side, and the opposite pit holds stones, you capture all stones from both pits and place them in your store.
